# Story: Create `wize-editorial-review-prose` and `wize-editorial-review-structure`

## Context

Peggy Carter is the tech-writer persona, but she has no callable skills. Wize artifacts (brief, PRD, ADR, gate) often have prose drift (passive voice, hedging, jargon) and structural drift (inconsistent sections, missing subsections). This story adds two skills for Peggy: one for prose, one for structure.

## Acceptance criteria

- **AC-E06-3:** Skill `src/core-skills/wize-editorial-review-prose/skill.md` exists with frontmatter `status: ready`.
- **AC-E06-4:** Skill `src/core-skills/wize-editorial-review-structure/skill.md` exists with frontmatter `status: ready`.
- Prose skill checks: voice (active preferred), jargon, hedging ("might", "could possibly"), ambiguous pronouns.
- Structure skill checks: section ordering, missing required sections, inconsistent headings.
- Both skills return a structured review with line-level findings.
- `npm test` remains green.
- Adapters IDE regenerated.

## Out of scope

- Auto-fix the prose (suggestions only; the human edits).
- Spelling/grammar (use a linter).
- Tone (this is technical writing; tone is intentional).
